Big-data analytics with R

1 week course on Big-data analytics with R 48 hours consisting of lectures, tutorials and exercise.

Course content:

- Introduction to data science and basics of Statistics.
- Hypothesis testing and Introduction to R.
- Data Wrangling in R.
- Predictive analytics and time series forecasting.
- Model deployment for real applications.

Materials:

Lecture handouts and open source material.

Learning outcomes:

- Basics of Statistics from which data analysis principle are derived.
- Connecting with database and importing real time data.
- Familiarize with different Libraries and Create scripts in R.
- Smart Visualization effect.
- Anomalies detection.
- Cost performance index interactive dashboard.
- Equipment performance on hourly, daily, weekly monthly and yearly.
- 3D reporting and automatic report generation.
- Develop predictive analysis forecasting application in R shiny.
Suitable for Power sector, Steel and related infrastructure industry companies.
